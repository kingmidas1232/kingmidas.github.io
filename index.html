Flow of the Concert

Starting

The host will have the opening remarks, starting with the introduction. For example, greeting them on a good day and asking them if they are comfortable in their position.

Blast

In the middle of the event each performer will go on to the stage and have a fun performance and make the audience feel that they are part of the blast. While having the performance people can eat at the food stalls provided by the organizer. Each food stall will charge each food, for example smoothies, pizzas, loaded fries.

Talking about donatios

Set up options for attendees to make additional donations during the event, either in personal or through digital means. During the event engage with the audience by sharing information about the cause and impact of their contribution.

Last Performance

After the last band performs, the host will hype the audience and will announce that the last artist that is going to perform then the last artist will have a gorgeous entrance. After the concert, thank the attendees, sponsors, and volunteers for their support. And provide updates on how much was raised and how it will be used.
